# Blog Workspace

- `ideas/` stores raw new article ideas and topic seeds.
- `drafts/` stores active new article drafts in progress.
- `published/` stores final approved new article versions maintained in this repo.
- `templates/` stores reusable article templates.
- `seo-audits/` stores SEO review notes for new in-progress articles.
- `existing/raw/` stores original copies of already-written articles imported from the live site or earlier sources.
- `existing/cleaned/` stores cleaned and normalized versions of existing articles for easier review and reuse.
- `existing/audits/` stores SEO and content audits for existing articles.
